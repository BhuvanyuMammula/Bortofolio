Welcome to OOB MUN

OOB Model United Nations is a student-led conference dedicated to fostering diplomacy, critical thinking, and meaningful dialogue. Bringing together passionate delegates from diverse backgrounds, OOB MUN provides a platform to debate global challenges, develop leadership skills, and collaborate on innovative solutions in an engaging and intellectually stimulating environment.
